COVID-19 Testing Process

Step 1: Registration

Please provide your personal information and consent to the COVID-19 testing process.

Step 2: Sample Collection

A healthcare professional will collect a sample, usually through a swab in your nose or throat.

Step 3: Sample Processing

The collected sample will be sent to the lab for processing and analysis.

Step 4: Test Result

You will receive your test result after a designated waiting period.
